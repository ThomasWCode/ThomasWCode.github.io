Music and Drama

Actually, I do have hobbies other than programming!

Music

From Year 5 in primary school, I have played piano and drums. However, I found drums much more suited to me and dropped piano in Year 7 after passing my Grade 2 with merit.

I continued to love drums, gaining my Grade 1, 2, 3, 4 and 5 certificates with 3 merits and 2 distinctions so far.

Currently I am not working on a grade, instead just playing different tracks, which I find is my favourite way to play drums.

At school, I am in band with 2 friends. We have performed at multiple concerts within the school. Below is us performing 'Sunday Bloody Sunday'.


Us performing Sunday Bloody Sunday at a school band festival

LAMDA

At school, I do LAMDA, which is an awarding body for drama. You perform either a solo monologue or a scene with a partner.

You are then graded just like for music. I have passed my Grade 1, 2, 3, 4, 5 and 6 (Bronze Medal) with 5 distinctions and 1 merit.
