About Me - An Overview

I'm 15 years old (Year 11 / 10th Grade), growing up in the center of London. My main interests are programming (and other tech stuff), astro and particle physics and hiking.

At school, my favourite subjects are Physics, DT (Design and Technology) and German. I do my GCSEs next year.

My favourite sport is running. I play drums and at school I'm in a band with two friends. I also do LAMDA.

My favourite fiction series is the Bobiverse series by Dennis E. Taylor (and his other stand-alone sci-fi novels).

My favourite non-fiction books are Stephen Hawking's 'A Brief History of Time' and 'Brief Answers to the Big Questions'.

I am currently reading (well, listening to) 'The Anxious Generation' by Jonathan Haidt.
