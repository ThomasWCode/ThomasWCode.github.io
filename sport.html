Sport

I've had the opportunity to play many different types of sport. My favourite are running, squash, tennis and football.

Running

I enjoy running and go to my local 5k Parkrun every saturday with friends. My current 5k PB is 21:59.

The last obstacle in the Spartan: the monkey bars

Spartan

I have been participating in a Spartan Race each year for 3 years with my dad and friends, which I really love. So far, I have done a Kids 2.5k, a 5k, and a 10k.

The races involve running a distance while overcoming 25 to 50 obstacles like walls, barbed wire, carrying sandbags and more. Highly recommended!

Brighton Triathlon

With the same friends, I do the Brighton triathlon each year. I find it really fun although the swim can be just a tiny bit cold :)

My Other Sports

I played squash between the ages of 8 to 13. Although I was coached for a few months, I mainly just played for fun. I took park in a few tournaments, ranking between 3rd and 6th.

Along with squash, I really enjoy tennis. I play at school during the summer term and play with friends at summer camps and sometimes on weekends.

Football has never been my best sport. I have only ever played it at school, finding myself in the E team (Teams are A-G).
